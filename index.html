📋 GAME RULES
OBJECTIVE

Race to colonize space! Earn Colony Points by deploying infrastructure at colony sites. First player to reach 10 pts with full crew health wins — or be the last crew standing.

TURN STRUCTURE

Each round, an Event Card plays at the start. Then each player takes 2 Actions. End your turn to apply Crew Decay.

ACTIONS

Move Forward — Advance along the path. Costs Funding equal to spaces moved × faction speed. Private Corp moves 3 spaces at once!

Move Back — Retreat 1 space at no cost. Useful if you overshot a colony site.

Rest — All crew gain +2 Sleep. Rest 2 turns in a row for a bonus: +1 Morale (costs 1 Funding) or –1 Repro Risk.

Buy Opportunity — Pay 2 Funding to draw an Opportunity Card. Play them from your hand at any time.

Deploy Infrastructure — At a colony or safe site, spend 3 consecutive turns deploying to earn Colony Points. Repro Risk tokens reduce points earned.

Research — Remove 1 Radiation from the most irradiated crew member, or remove 1 Repro Risk token.

Negotiate — Gain 2 Funding.

BOARD SPACES

Earth (0) — Starting position. No hazards.

Launch Window (1) — Gain +1 Funding on arrival.

Low Earth Orbit (2) — All crew –1 Sleep on arrival.

I.S.S. (3) — Safe. Deploy here for 2 Colony Points.

Lunar Transit (4) — Triggers an Event Card on arrival.

The Moon (5) — Colony site (5 pts). Grants Policy Shield. Prompt appears to land when passing.

Asteroid Belt (6) — All crew +1 Radiation on arrival.

Mars Approach (7) — Triggers an Event Card on arrival.

Mars (8) — Colony site (12 pts). All crew +1 Radiation, –1 Morale. Prompt appears to land when passing.

Deep Space (9) — Colony site (20 pts). All crew +2 Radiation, –2 Morale, –1 Sleep.

CREW HEALTH TRACKS

☢ Radiation (0–5) — Increases in deep space. At max, adds a Repro Risk token. Cannot be rested away.

𝗓ᶻ Sleep (0–5) — Depletes each turn in space. At ≤1: Commander limits you to 1 Action; Engineer raises Move cost.

⏻ Morale (0–5) — Drops from isolation. At 0: triggers a Mental Breakdown card. Commander at 0 draws a Mutiny.

CARD TYPES

Event Cards — Drawn once per round at round start. Affect all or some players.

Policy Cards — Triggered when a new location is first reached. Affect all factions.

Opportunity Cards — Bought for 2 Funding. Beneficial — play from hand any time during your turn.

Mental Breakdown Cards — Drawn when a crew member hits 0 Morale. Bad news.

FACTIONS

NASA — SPD 1 · 10 Funding · Strong policy protection. Can ignore one Policy penalty per round.

CNSA — SPD 2 · 8 Funding · Medium protection. Can delay one Policy effect by 1 turn.

ESA — SPD 1 · 8 Funding · Immune to Policy Cards entirely.

Private Corp — SPD 3 · 12 Funding · No protection. Moves fast but Policy Cards hurt.

WIN CONDITIONS

Primary Victory — First to reach 10+ Colony Points with all crew at full Sleep and Morale.

Last Standing — If all other factions' crews go critical, you win.

Global Catastrophe — If the Solar Superflare event triggers and no one holds Opportunity Cards, everyone loses.
